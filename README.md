# NewsBiasDetection
IBM research project
 Worked on mining bias in articles of different news sources. Scrapped articles and used an attention network to find
words of high importance/attention for calculating bias. Proposed a sensationalism score which combined several
aspects of the article such as word centrality, article sentiment and word attention.
 Applied clustering and dimension reduction algorithms to identify abnormal representation of topics in news article.
Created visualizations for showing topic representation and bias in articles. Paper published, AAAI 2020 (SA).
